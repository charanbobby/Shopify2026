    Requirements Backlog¶

    Source: Shopify_requirements_worksheet v4.xlsx - Requirements sheet

    Must Have¶

    Must Have - Customer & Checkout Experience¶

    • [5.1] Gift Card Purchase & Redemption
    • [5.2] Flexible Promo Code & Discount Engine
    • [5.4] Maintain CA & Expand US Payment Methods
    • [5.5] Shop Pay (Accelerated Checkout) Implementation
    • [5.10] Enable Single-Order Checkout for Multiple Delivery Methods

    Must Have - Customer Data, Service & B2B¶

    • [6.1] Zendesk Integration for Order Data
    • [6.2] ClaimLane Returns/Warranty Integration
    • [6.5] Zowie Chatbot Integration
    • [6.7] Integrate Key B2B Partners via Dscopify App or other alternative solution to Shopify

    Must Have - Data & Content Migration¶

    • [2.1] Migrate Secondary Site (bestsleepsociety) Content
    • [2.2] Maintain Existing SEO Performance & Redirects

    Must Have - Fulfillment, Inventory & Operations¶

    • [4.1] Fulfil Integration: Replicate Existing Order/Inventory Flows
    • [4.2] Trackship Integration or Explore Alternatives
    • [4.3] Shipment & Return Tracking Optimization
    • [4.6] BOPIS/Omni-Channel Implementation

    Must Have - Marketing & Engagement¶

    • [7.2] Shopify Collabs Influencer Platform Setup
    • [7.5] Preserve Review Integration with Google Business Profile
    • [7.6] Impact Radius (impact.com) Tracking Migration
    • [7.7] Sprout Social Migration & Link Integrity
    • [7.8] Select & Implement New UGC/Review Platform {Keep RaveCapture OR Select New Platform (Judge.me/Junip)}
    • [7.10] Active Campaign or Klaviyo Migration/Integration

    Must Have - Platform & Environment Setup¶

    • [1.1] Shopify Plus Environment Configuration
    • [1.2] Launch US E-commerce Platform (Shopify Markets)
    • [1.3] Launch Canadian E-commerce Platform (English/French)

    Must Have - Product Management & Logic¶

    • [3.2] Migrate SKU Builder & Bundle Configuration Logic
    • [3.5] Bundle Product Setup & Component Mapping
    • [3.6] CSV Bulk Product Import Tool

    Should Have - Marketing & Engagement¶

    • [7.4] Loyalty & Rewards Program Implementation

    Should Have - Product Management & Logic¶
